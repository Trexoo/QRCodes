Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first QR code? +

Creating a QR code is simple:

  1. Go to the QR Generator
  2. Select the QR code type (URL, text, email, etc.)
  3. Enter your content in the form fields
  4. Customize colors and size if desired
  5. Click "Generate QR Code"
  6. Download in your preferred format
How do I upgrade my subscription plan? +

To upgrade your plan:

  1. Log in to your Dashboard
  2. Click on "Account Settings" or visit the Pricing page
  3. Select your desired plan
  4. Complete the payment process

Your new plan features will be available immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the current billing period.

How do I get my API key? +

API access is available on Pro and higher plans. To get your API key:

  1. Go to your API Keys page
  2. Click "Create New Key"
  3. Name your key and set permissions
  4. Copy and securely store your API key

See our API Documentation for integration guides.

What QR code types are supported? +

QRCore supports 13+ QR code types:

  • URL: Link to websites
  • Text: Plain text messages
  • Email: Pre-filled email composition
  • Phone: Click-to-call phone numbers
  • SMS: Pre-filled text messages
  • WiFi: Network connection details
  • vCard: Contact information cards
  • GS1: Industry-standard product codes
  • UPC/EAN: Barcode formats
  • Event: Calendar events
  • Location: GPS coordinates
  • Bitcoin: Cryptocurrency payments
How does QR code tracking work? +

QRCore tracking (available on Pro and higher plans) works by creating dynamic QR codes that redirect through our servers. This allows us to capture:

  • Total and unique scan counts
  • Geographic location (city/country level)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Time and date of scans
  • Referrer information

View analytics in your Dashboard.

My QR code isn't scanning. What should I do? +

If your QR code isn't scanning, try these steps:

  1. Size: Ensure the QR code is at least 2cm x 2cm (0.8in x 0.8in)
  2. Contrast: Use high contrast colors (dark foreground, light background)
  3. Quality: Download in PNG or SVG format for best quality
  4. Error Correction: Use "High" error correction for printed materials
  5. Content: Verify the encoded content is valid and accessible
  6. Lighting: Avoid glare or shadows when scanning

If issues persist, contact our support team.

Can I cancel my subscription? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Go to your Dashboard ‚Üí Account Settings
  2. Click "Manage Subscription"
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"

Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. Your QR codes will remain functional, but you'll lose access to premium features after cancellation.
